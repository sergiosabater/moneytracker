**Money Tracker** is a Flutter application designed to help users manage their personal finances efficiently. Track your income and expenses, visualize your spending habits, and gain insights to make smarter financial decisions.

---

## Features

- **Expense & Income Management**: Easily add, edit, and delete transactions.
- **Categories**: Organize your transactions with customizable categories (e.g., Food, Transport, Entertainment).
- **Graphs & Analytics**: Visualize your financial habits with pie charts, bar charts, and line graphs.
- **Budget Tracking**: Set monthly budgets and monitor your progress.
- **Recurring Transactions**: Automatically track recurring payments or income.
- **Dark & Light Mode**: Seamless theme switching for comfortable use anytime.
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Track expenses in different currencies.
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find transactions by date, category, or amount.
- **Secure Data**: Optional local authentication (PIN or biometric) to protect your financial data.
- **Backup & Restore**: Export and import your data for safety and migration.
